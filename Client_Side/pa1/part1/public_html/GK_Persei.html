Supernova Remnant: GK Persei

A supernova remnant in the constellation Perseus. Unlike a crab or composite type, it does not emit a large amount of X-ray or gamma radiation. As a shell-type supernova remnant, it displays the following properties:

  • Width: 0.7 Lightyears
  • Supernova Date: 1901 AD
  • Supernova Type: White Dwarf Nova
  • Emission: None
  • Shells: Yes, asymmetrical
  • Point Source: White Dwarf, Red Giant
