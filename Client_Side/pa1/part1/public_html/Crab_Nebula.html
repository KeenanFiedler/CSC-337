Supernova Remnant: Crab Nebula

A supernova remnant in the constellation Taurus. Unlike a shell or composite type, it does not have a clear shell of material, beign mostly filamentary strands around the center. As the prototypical crab-type supernova remnant, it displays the following properties:

  • Width: 11 Lightyears
  • Supernova Date: 1054 AD
  • Supernova Type: Electron-Capture
  • Emission: X-ray and Gamma emission
  • Shells: None, Filamentary
  • Point Source: Neutron Star
