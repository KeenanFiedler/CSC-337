Supernova Remnant: IC-443

A supernova remnant in the constellation Gemini. This composite type supernova remant displays a combination of shell-type and crab-type supernovae remnants. It displays the following properties:

  • Width: 70 Lightyears
  • Supernova Date: 3000-30000 years ago
  • Supernova Type: II
  • Emission: X-ray emission
  • Shells: 2 sub-shells connected
  • Point Source: Neutron Star
